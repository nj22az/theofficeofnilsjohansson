## 1. Purpose

This document provides a ready-to-use inventory register for pressurised vessels compliant with **AFS 2017:3** (Arbetsmiljöverket). It is intended for use by operators (brukare) who need to maintain a **förteckning** (register) of all pressurised equipment on site.

The inventory satisfies:
- **AFS 2017:3** — Swedish regulation for use and inspection of pressurised equipment
- **PED 2014/68/EU** — Pressure Equipment Directive (as implemented in Sweden via AFS 2016:2)
- **ISO 9001:2015 clause 7.1.5** — Monitoring and measuring resources

> **Lagkrav:** Enligt AFS 2017:3 ska brukaren föra en förteckning över alla trycksatta anordningar som omfattas av föreskrifterna. Förteckningen ska hållas aktuell och vara tillgänglig för tillsynsmyndigheten.

## 2. Regulatory Classification — AFS 2017:3

### 2.1 Scope

AFS 2017:3 applies to pressurised equipment that meets the following criteria:

| Equipment type | Threshold for inclusion |
|---------------|------------------------|
| **Pressure vessels** | PS > 0.5 bar and PS × V > 50 bar·L |
| **Piping** | PS > 0.5 bar and PS × DN > 2000 bar·mm |
| **Steam/hot water boilers** | PS > 0.5 bar |

Equipment below these thresholds is **not** covered by AFS 2017:3 but may still be subject to general workplace safety requirements (AML).

### 2.2 Classification (Klass A and Klass B)

| Klass | Criteria | Inspection requirement |
|-------|----------|----------------------|
| **Klass A** | Higher risk — per AFS 2017:3 tables based on PS, V, medium, and fluid group | Must be inspected by an **ackrediterat kontrollorgan** (accredited inspection body: Kiwa, DEKRA, DNV, TÜV, Bureau Veritas, Apave) |
| **Klass B** | Lower risk — below Klass A thresholds but above AFS 2017:3 scope threshold | May be inspected by the operator's **own competent personnel** with documented competence |

### 2.3 Fluid Groups (PED 2014/68/EU, Article 13)

| Group | Definition | Examples |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| **Group 1** | Dangerous fluids — explosive, flammable, toxic, oxidising | Ammonia, LPG, acetylene, chlorine, hydrogen |
| **Group 2** | All other fluids not in Group 1 | Compressed air, nitrogen, water, steam, hydraulic oil |

### 2.4 PED Categories (for reference)

| Category | Risk level | Conformity assessment |
|----------|-----------|----------------------|
| **Art. 4.3** | Below PED threshold — no CE marking required | Sound engineering practice |
| **Category I** | Low risk | Internal production control (Module A) |
| **Category II** | Medium risk | Internal + monitoring (Module A2, D1, E1) |
| **Category III** | Higher risk | Type examination + production QA (Module B+D, B+F, etc.) |
| **Category IV** | Highest risk | Full QA + type examination (Module B+D, B+F, G, H1) |

## 3. Inspection Requirements — AFS 2017:3

### 3.1 Inspection Types

| Swedish term | English | Description |
|-------------|---------|-------------|
| **Första kontroll** | First inspection | Before first use or after major repair/modification |
| **Återkommande kontroll — Utvändig** | Periodic — External | Visual external examination |
| **Återkommande kontroll — Invändig (revision)** | Periodic — Internal (revision) | Internal examination of pressure-retaining parts |
| **Återkommande kontroll — Tryckprov** | Periodic — Pressure test | Hydraulic or pneumatic pressure test |
| **Skadekontroll** | Damage inspection | After damage, incident, or abnormal operating event |

### 3.2 Standard Inspection Intervals

| Equipment type | External (utvändig) | Internal revision (invändig) | Pressure test (tryckprov) |
|---------------|--------------------|-----------------------------|---------------------------|
| **Ångpannor (steam boilers)** | 2 years | 4 years | 8 years |
| **Tryckkärl Klass A** | 2 years | 6 years | 12 years |
| **Tryckkärl Klass B** | 2 years (operator) | 6 years (operator) | — |
| **Rörledningar Klass A** | 2 years | 6 years | 12 years |
| **Rörledningar Klass B** | 2 years (operator) | — | — |

**Notes:**
- Intervals may be **extended** with documented risk assessment approved by the accredited inspection body
- Intervals may be **shortened** for corrosive service, high-temperature hydrogen attack, CUI risk, or other degradation mechanisms
- After repair or alteration, a new **första kontroll** may be required

### 3.3 Operator Obligations (Brukarens skyldigheter)

Under AFS 2017:3, the operator shall:

1. Maintain an **up-to-date förteckning** (this document)
2. Ensure all inspections are performed **on time** by qualified parties
3. Keep all documentation **accessible** — inspection reports, certificates, drawings
4. Perform regular **tillsyn** (supervision/monitoring) between formal inspections
5. Report incidents to **Arbetsmiljöverket**
6. Ensure personnel are **competent** (documented training per JDS-PRO-009)
7. Have **written routines** for operation, maintenance, and emergency procedures

## 7. Accredited Inspection Bodies in Sweden

The following bodies are accredited by **Swedac** for Klass A inspections:

| Organisation | Common scope |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Kiwa Inspecta** | All pressure equipment |
| **DEKRA Industrial** | All pressure equipment |
| **DNV** | Pressure vessels, piping, boilers |
| **TÜV Nord / TÜV Süd** | All pressure equipment |
| **Bureau Veritas** | All pressure equipment |
| **Apave** | All pressure equipment |

Contact your accredited body to schedule inspections and discuss interval adjustments.

## 1. Purpose

This document maps every requirement in the JDS Ongoing Maintenance Program (JDS-PRO-010) back to the specific sections of **AFS 2017:3** (Swedish regulation for use and inspection of pressurised equipment) that it satisfies.

JDS-PRO-010 is the authoritative working procedure. This matrix is the evidence trail that proves the procedure meets regulatory requirements. The two documents are deliberately separate: the procedure is clean and operational; this matrix is the compliance reference.

---

## 2. How to Use This Document

- **During audits:** Show this matrix to demonstrate that every regulatory requirement is covered
- **When updating PRO-010:** Check this matrix to ensure no regulatory requirement is lost
- **When adapting to other countries:** Create a new traceability matrix mapping to the other country's regulation

## 11. Swedish Terminology Reference

This section provides the Swedish-to-English mapping for regulatory terms encountered in AFS 2017:3. JDS documents use the English equivalents exclusively.

| Swedish Term | JDS English Term | Context |
|---|---|---|
| Fortlöpande tillsyn | Ongoing supervision / Ongoing maintenance | The operator's continuous maintenance responsibility |
| Tryckkärl | Pressure vessel | Closed vessel designed to hold fluid under pressure |
| Brukare | Operator | Person or organisation responsible for the equipment |
| Ackrediterat kontrollorgan | Accredited inspection body | Third-party inspector (DEKRA, Kiwa, RISE, etc.) |
| Egen kontroll | Own inspection | Inspection by operator's competent personnel |
| Utvändig undersökning | External examination | Visual inspection of vessel exterior |
| Invändig undersökning | Internal examination | Examination of vessel interior |
| Tryckprov | Pressure test | Hydrostatic or pneumatic proof test |
| Kontrollintyg | Inspection certificate | Official document issued after inspection |
| Kontrollplan | Inspection plan | Annual schedule of inspections and supervision |
| Tillsynsprotokoll | Supervision checklist | Record of routine walk-around supervision |
| Godkänd | Approved | Inspection result — vessel may continue in service |
| Godkänd med anmärkning | Approved with remarks | Inspection result — findings require attention |
| Underkänd | Rejected | Inspection result — vessel must be taken out of service |
| Klass A / Klass B | Class A / Class B | Risk classification determining inspection requirements |
| Säkerhetsventil | Safety valve | Pressure relief device |
| Arbetsmiljöverket | Swedish Work Environment Authority | National regulatory authority |
| SWEDAC | Swedish Board for Accreditation | Accredits inspection bodies |

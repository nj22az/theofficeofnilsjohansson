## 1. Purpose

This risk assessment identifies hazards, causes, consequences, and existing safeguards for all pressurised vessels at [site name]. It uses a Probability x Consequence matrix (5 x 5) to rate each risk and determine whether additional controls are needed.

This assessment satisfies the requirement in AFS 2017:3, 2 Kap. §1 to assess risks considering:

1. Experience from use of the equipment
2. Information about remaining lifetime
3. Repairs and modifications performed
4. Accidents and incidents that have occurred
5. Deviation reports and inspection results

## 2. Risk Matrix

### 2.1 Probability Scale

| Score | Level | Description |
|:-----:|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Very unlikely | Has never occurred; highly unusual scenario |
| 2 | Unlikely | Has occurred elsewhere; possible but rare |
| 3 | Possible | Has occurred at this site or similar; can happen |
| 4 | Likely | Has occurred more than once; expected over time |
| 5 | Very likely | Occurs regularly or is expected soon |

### 2.2 Consequence Scale

| Score | Level | Personnel Injury | Environmental / Property |
|:-----:|-------|-----------------|------------------------|
| 1 | Negligible | No injury | Negligible damage |
| 2 | Minor | Minor injury, first aid | Minor damage, contained |
| 3 | Moderate | Medical treatment, lost time | Significant damage, localised |
| 4 | Major | Serious injury, hospitalisation | Major damage, wider area |
| 5 | Catastrophic | Fatal or life-changing injury | Catastrophic, regulatory reportable |

### 2.3 Risk Rating (P x C)

| | C=1 | C=2 | C=3 | C=4 | C=5 |
|---|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| **P=5** | 5 | 10 | 15 | 20 | **25** |
| **P=4** | 4 | 8 | 12 | 16 | **20** |
| **P=3** | 3 | 6 | 9 | 12 | **15** |
| **P=2** | 2 | 4 | 6 | 8 | **10** |
| **P=1** | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | **5** |

| Rating | Action |
|--------|--------|
| **1-4** (Low) | Acceptable — monitor through supervision program |
| **5-9** (Medium) | Acceptable with controls — ensure safeguards are maintained |
| **10-15** (High) | Action required — implement additional controls |
| **16-25** (Critical) | Immediate action — stop operation until mitigated |
